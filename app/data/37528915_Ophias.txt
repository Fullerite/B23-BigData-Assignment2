Ophias is a monotypic snout moth genus. Its only species, Ophias albiundalis, was described by Émile Louis Ragonot in February 1891. It is found in Pernambuco, Brazil."Ophias Ragonot, 1891" at Markku Savela's Lepidoptera and Some Other Life Forms.
