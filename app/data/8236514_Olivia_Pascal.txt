Olivia Pascal (born Olivia Gerlitzki: 26 May 1957 in Munich, West Germany) is a German actress. == Career == Olivia Pascal was working as a medical assistant in 1976 when she was discovered by director Hubert Frank for the erotic film Vanessa. She then played in several erotic films and sex comedies such as The Fruit is Ripe (1977), Interno di un convento (1977) and Die Insel der tausend Freuden (aka Triangle of Venus) (1978) as well as horror films and thrillers before becoming a television actress. She appeared as a presenter on the television series Bananas from 1981 to 1984. She went on to regular roles on multiple German television series: Somehow and Anyways in 1986, The Black Forest Clinic from 1986 to 1989, Friends for Life from 1994 to 1997, and the telenovela Verliebt in Berlin from 2005 to 2007. She had a long-running role as Detective Inspector Lizzy Berger in SOKO 5113 from 1988 to 1997.
