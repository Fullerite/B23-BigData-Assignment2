Ocean Ranger is a 1988 video game published by Activision. ==Gameplay== Ocean Ranger is a game in which several war zones are involved in naval combat. ==Reception== David Wilson reviewed the game for Computer Gaming World, and stated that "While Ocean Ranger should by no means be considered a "serious" wartime simulation, it can certainly be considered "serious" fun.
