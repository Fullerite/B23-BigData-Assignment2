Olga Čechová (6 April 1925 – 24 November 2010) was a Czech printmaker. A native of Brno, Čechová studied at the School of Arts and Crafts in that city from 1941 until 1944. From 1946 until 1951 she was a student at the Academy of Arts, Architecture and Design in Prague, where she was a pupil of Karel Svolinský and . She was active mainly as an illustrator, and her work appeared in many magazines and books, both for children and for adults.
