The One More HAIM Tour was the third headlining tour by the American pop rock band Haim in support of their third studio album Women in Music Pt. III (2020). The 45-stop tour began on April 24, 2022, in Las Vegas, Nevada at the Cosmopolitan and ended on September 24, 2022, at Firefly Music Festival in Dover, Delaware. ==Background== On December 6, 2021, Haim announced a 27-date North American tour via a video on their Twitter of the sisters dancing to "What Dreams Are Made Of" from The Lizzie McGuire Movie (2003) followed by an announcement of a tour with dates and venues listed at the end. Several stops on their European leg were delayed dates from a planned 2021 tour, which was postponed due to COVID-19-restrictions at the time. Two stops on the tour featured the band opening for the Red Hot Chili Peppers on their 2022 Global Stadium Tour. ==Setlist== This set list is representative of the performance on May 31, 2022.
