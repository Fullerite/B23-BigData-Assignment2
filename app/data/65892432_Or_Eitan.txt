Or Eitan (אור איתן; born November 9, 1981) is an Israeli former basketball player. He was named to the 2006 Israeli Basketball Premier League Quintet. ==Biography== He was born in Israel. Eitan is 6' 4" (193 cm) tall. Eitan played the forward position. He played for Cholet Basket, Hapoel Gilboa Galil, Hapoel Tel Aviv, Elitzur Ashkelon, and Maccabi Rishon LeZion.
