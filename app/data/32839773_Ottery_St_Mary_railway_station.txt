Ottery St Mary railway station is a closed railway station that served the town of Ottery St Mary, in Devon, England. It was opened in 1874 on the Sidmouth Railway, but was closed in 1967 due to the Beeching Axe. ==History== thumb|left|Local railway lines The station originally opened as Ottery St Mary town, probably to distinguish from , which was originally named Ottery St Mary and Ottery road. Passenger and freight services were withdrawn in 1967. ==Present state== The station building is now a youth club.
