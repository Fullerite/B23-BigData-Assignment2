Olympique Batna is an Algerian basketball club based in Batna. Established in 1972, the team plays in the Algerian Basketball Championship. ==History== The team was founded by Brahim Yezza, Khomri Miloud and Kaouli Chérif in 1972. ==Notable players== * Mamadou Faye ==References== Category:Basketball teams in Algeria Category:Basketball teams established in 1972
