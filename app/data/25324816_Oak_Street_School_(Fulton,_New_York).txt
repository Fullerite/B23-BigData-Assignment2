Oak Street School is a historic school building located at Fulton in Oswego County, New York. It is a rectangular red brick structure, trimmed in cast stone and built in 1913–1914. It is a two-story building over a high brick basement. It ceased being used as a school in 1991. See also: It has since been retrofitted into small apartments used primarily by senior residents of the city.
