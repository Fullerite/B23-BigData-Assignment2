Oilinyphia is a genus of Asian dwarf spiders that was first described by H. Ono & H. Saito in 1989. ==Species== it contains three species: *Oilinyphia hengji Zhao & Li, 2014 – China *Oilinyphia jadbounorum Ponksee & Tanikawa, 2010 – Thailand *Oilinyphia peculiaris Ono & Saito, 1989 (type) – Japan (Ryukyu Is.) ==See also== * List of Linyphiidae species (I–P) ==References== Category:Araneomorphae genera Category:Linyphiidae Category:Spiders of Asia
