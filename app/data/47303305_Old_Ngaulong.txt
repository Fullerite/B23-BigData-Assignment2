Old Ngaulong is a village in the Peren district of Nagaland, India. It is located in the Nsong Circle. == Demographics == According to the 2011 census of India, Old Ngaulong has 102 households. The effective literacy rate (i.e. the literacy rate of population excluding children aged 6 and below) is 70.68%.
