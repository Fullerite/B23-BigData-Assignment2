Oomakkuyil Padumbol () is a 2012 Indian Malayalam-language children's film produced and directed by Siddique Chennamangalloor, starring Malavika Nair, Akash Roshan, Shankar and Sangeetha Rajendran. ==Plot== The story is about Reema (Malavika Nair), a young Muslim schoolgirl in Malabar, who loved Malayalam and poetry.
