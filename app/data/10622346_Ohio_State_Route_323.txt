State Route 323 (SR 323) is an east-west state highway in southwestern Ohio. SR 323's western terminus is at SR 41 in South Solon. Its eastern terminus is at SR 56 just northwest of Mount Sterling. ==Route description== thumb|left|At the western terminus in South Solon SR 323 runs exclusively within the southern portion of Madison County.
