OrangeHRM Inc. is a HR software company based in Secaucus, New Jersey, best known for its Human resource management system. OrangeHRM was founded by Sujee Saparamadu in 2005. OrangeHRM offers starter and advanced version for its clients. ==History== Founder and CEO of OrangeHRM Sujee Saparamadu, started OrangeHRM in 2005 with four employees. OrangeHRM 1.0 was a free and open source version released to Sourceforge in March 2006. 1.0 comprised features such as employee information management, employee self-service, and reporting. In 2010 OrangeHRM received a Series A investment from principal investors Larry Stefonic and David Axmark. David Axmark.
