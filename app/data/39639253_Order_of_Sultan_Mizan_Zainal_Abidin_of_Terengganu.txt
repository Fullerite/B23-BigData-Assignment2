The Most Select Order of Sultan Mizan Zainal Abidin of Terengganu (Bahasa Melayu: Darjah Kebesaran Sultan Mizan Zainal Abidin Terengganu Yang Amat Terpilih) is an honorific order of the Sultanate of Terengganu == History == It was founded by Sultan Mizan Zainal Abidin of Terengganu on 6 July 2001. == Classes == It is awarded in a supreme class: * Sri Utama (established 26 May 2005) - S.U.M.Z. and four ordinary classes : # Knight Grand Companion or Dato' Sri Setia - S.S.M.Z. # Knight Companion or Dato' Setia - D.S.M.Z. # Companion or Setia - S.M.Z. # Member or Ahli - A.M.Z.
