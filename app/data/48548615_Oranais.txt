Oranais is a cultural region of the Maghreb, located in northwestern Algeria. ==Geography== It corresponds roughly to the following wilayas: *Oran Province *Mostaganem Province *Mascara Province *Sidi Bel Abbès Province *Relizane Province *Aïn Témouchent Province *Tlemcen Province.
