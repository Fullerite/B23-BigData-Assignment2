Ornativalva ornatella is a moth of the family Gelechiidae. It was described by Sattler in 1967. It is found in Romania, the southern part of European Russia, Turkey, Iran, Afghanistan and Mongolia.funet.fiFauna Europaea Adults have been recorded on wing from May to July, and in August.
