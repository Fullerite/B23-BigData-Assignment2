Oak Grove High School is a secondary school located in the Edenvale neighborhood of San Jose, California, United States, which serves students in grades 9–12. Average enrollment is 1800 students, compared to the state average of 1413. The school is part of the East Side Union High School District and its mascot is the eagle.
