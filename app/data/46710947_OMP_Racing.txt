OMP Racing Spa (Officine Meccaniche Percivale) is a manufacturer of motorsport safety equipment and racing car accessories founded in 1973 and based in the area of Genoa, Italy. == History == thumb|Racing gloves by OMP The company was founded by the three Percivale brothers (Claudio, Piergiorgio and Roberto) in 1973 and initially produced only rally car accessories such as rollbars and tail pipes. In the Eighties the company began producing equipment for drivers (suits, gloves, boots, helmets etc.). Since 2008 the company's majority share is owned by brothers Paolo and Alberto Delprato.
