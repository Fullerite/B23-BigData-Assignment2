Our Private Lives () is a Canadian drama film, directed by Denis Côté and released in 2007.Maurie Alioff, "Denis Côté". The Canadian Encyclopedia, August 21, 2014. The film centres on the relationship between Milena (Anastassia Liutova), a Bulgarian immigrant who has lived in Montreal for a decade, and Phillip (Penko Gospodinov), a photographer from Sofia, when they meet for the first time after several months of corresponding on the Internet.Alissa Simon, "Our Private Lives: Lovers run amuck in the wilderness in experimental drama 'Our Private Lives'. Variety, August 13, 2007. Côté wrote the screenplay in French, and it was translated into Bulgarian by the actors.Kevin Laforest, "Nos vies privées : Les amours mortes". Voir, November 22, 2007.
