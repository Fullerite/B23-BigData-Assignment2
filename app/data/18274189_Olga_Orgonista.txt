Olga Orgonista (born 22 February 1901 in Budapest, died 20 November 1978 in Budapest) was a Hungarian pair skater. With partner Sándor Szalay, she was the 1930 and 1931 European Champion. They won two medals at the World Figure Skating Championships, a bronze in 1929 and a silver in 1931.
