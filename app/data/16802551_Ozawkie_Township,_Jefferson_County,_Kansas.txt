Ozawkie Township is a township in Jefferson County, Kansas, USA. As of the 2000 census, its population was 1,408. ==Geography== Ozawkie Township covers an area of 42.78 square miles (110.79 square kilometers); of this, 5.45 square miles (14.11 square kilometers) or 12.74 percent is water.
