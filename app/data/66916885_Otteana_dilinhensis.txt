Otteana dilinhensis is a species of cricket in the subfamily Landrevinae, found in Vietnam. == Systematics == The species was first described in 1988 by Daniel Otte and placed under Pteroplistinae as Pteroplistus dilinhensis. A reassessment in 1990 by moved it to Otteana. == References == Category:Crickets Category:Orthoptera of Indo-China Category:Insects described in 1988
