Oh, What a Mighty Time is an album by the country rock band the New Riders of the Purple Sage. Their sixth studio album and their seventh album overall, it was released by Columbia Records in 1975.Oh, What a Mighty Time at the NRPS official website. Oh, What a Mighty Time was produced by Bob Johnston.
