One Night Stand is a Dutch anthology series that ran from 2004 till 2019 and was broadcast by the VPRO. Every episode was produced by a different production company and directed by a different director. Some of the directors made their film debut in this series, like Urszula Antoniak, Boudewijn Koole and Jaap van Heusden. ==Prizes== The series managed to win a lot of Golden Calves through the years. Six times they won the Golden Calf for Best Television Drama for the episodes: Den Helder in 2008, Anvers in 2009, Finnemans in 2010, Vast in 2011, Geen koningen in ons bloed in 2015 and Horizon in 2016.
