Oleksandr Ivaniukhin () is a Paralympian athlete from Ukraine competing mainly in category T11 sprint events. He competed in the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia. There he won a silver medal in the men's Pentathlon - P11 event, a bronze medal in the men's 100 metres - T11 event, finished fifth in the men's Long jump - F11 event and finished fifth in the men's Triple jump - F11 event. He also competed at the 2004 Summer Paralympics in Athens, Greece. There he won a bronze medal in the men's 200 metres - T11 event, finished fourth in the men's 100 metres - T11 event and finished fourth in the men's 400 metres - T11 event.
