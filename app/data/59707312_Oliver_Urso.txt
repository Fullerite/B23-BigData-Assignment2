Oliver Urso (born 29 April 1999) is a Danish football player who plays as a midfielder for Italian club Novara. ==Career== Urso started playing football in BK Søllerød-Vedbæk and later joined FC Copenhagen still as a youth player.Dansk stortalent slås for karrieren i Italien: Det har ikke været nemt, ekstrabaldet.dk, 6 March 2017 Urso then traveled to Italy with his Danish mom and Italian father when he was nine and settled in Rome, where he later joined AS Roma. He played for AS Roma for a few years and later one year at Gsd Nuova Tor Tre Teste, but chose to take the plunge and travel north to Cesena - without the family - after AC Cesena had shown interest in him. After a year alone in the small town on the Adriatic, he was joined by both his brother and parents who moved north to support him.
