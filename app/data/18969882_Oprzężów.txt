Oprzężów is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wola Krzysztoporska, within Piotrków County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately north of Wola Krzysztoporska, west of Piotrków Trybunalski, and south of the regional capital Łódź. ==References== Category:Villages in Piotrków County
