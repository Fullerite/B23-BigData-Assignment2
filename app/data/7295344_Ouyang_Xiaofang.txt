Ouyang Xiaofang (Simplified Chinese: 欧阳晓芳; born 5 April 1983) is a weightlifter from Liaoning, People's Republic of China. Ouyang participated in the women's -63 kg class at the 2006 World Weightlifting Championships and won the gold medal, snatching 110 kg and clean and jerking an additional 136 kg for a total of 246 kg.75th MEN'S and 18th WOMEN'S WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS -63 KG WOMEN , iwf.net At the 2006 Asian Games, she suffered a knee injury, but won the silver medal in the 63 kg category. ==Achievements== :Image:Med 1.png 2006 World Weightlifting Championships, -63 kg ==References== Category:Living people Category:1983 births Category:World Weightlifting Championships medalists Category:Weightlifters from Liaoning Category:Place of birth missing (living people) Category:Asian Games medalists in weightlifting Category:Weightlifters at the 2006 Asian Games Category:Chinese female weightlifters Category:Female powerlifters Category:People from Chaoyang, Liaoning Category:Asian Games silver medalists for China Category:Medalists at the 2006 Asian Games Category:21st-century Chinese women
