Olivier Peslier (born 12 January 1973 in Château-Gontier) is a French thoroughbred horse racing jockey.Olivier Peslier—Champion in France Peslier competes in flat racing mainly in France but travels often around the world for the big international races. He was a retained jockey for Wertheimer et Frère from 2003 to 2014.
