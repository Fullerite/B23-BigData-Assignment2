Osadcheye () is a rural locality (a selo) in Alexeyevsky District, Belgorod Oblast, Russia. The population was 234 as of 2010. There are 3 streets. == Geography == Osadcheye is located 39 km south of Alexeyevka (the district's administrative centre) by road.
