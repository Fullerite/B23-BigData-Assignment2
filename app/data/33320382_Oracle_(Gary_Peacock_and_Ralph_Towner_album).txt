Oracle is an album by American jazz bassist Gary Peacock and guitarist Ralph Towner, recorded in 1993 and released on the ECM label.ECM discography accessed October 6, 2011 == Reception == The AllMusic review by Scott Yanow stated: "There is a surprising amount of ferocious interplay between the two musicians. They may play at a consistently low volume, but the set of originals has a few rather passionate grooves and a little more energy than one would have predicted".Yanow, S.
