Oldeholtwolde () is a village in Weststellingwerf in the province of Friesland, the Netherlands. It had a population of around 150 in 2017.Villages - Weststellingwerf municipality The village was first mentioned in 1320 as Oldeholtwolt, and means "old low-lying wood". Olde (old) has been added to distinguish from Nijeholtwolde. The Dutch Reformed church was destroyed in a storm around 1700. A new church was built in 1875.
