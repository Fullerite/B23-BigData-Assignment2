Olaf Everson (6 February 1912 - 27 November 1995) was a New Zealand cricketer. He played one first-class match for Otago in 1943/44. Everson was born at Auckland in 1912. His occupation was a "preacher". He died at Hamilton in 1995; an obituary was published in the New Zealand Cricket Almanack the following year.McCarron A (2010) New Zealand Cricketers 1863/64–2010, p. 49.
