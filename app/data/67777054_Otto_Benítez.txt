Otto Benítez is a Uruguayan chess player, two-times Uruguayan Chess Championship winner (1974, 1975). ==Biography== In the 1960s and 1970s Otto Benítez was one of Uruguayan leading chess players. He won three medals in Uruguayan Chess Championships: 2 gold (1974, 1975) and silver (1962). In 1975, in Fortaleza Otto Benítez participated in World Chess Championship South American Zonal tournament. Otto Benítez played for Uruguay in the Chess Olympiad: * In 1976, at first board in the 22nd Chess Olympiad in Haifa (+4, =2, -4).
