Obren Čučković (; born 2 May 1983) is a Serbian former football goalkeeper. ==Career== Born in Bač, Čuković played for OFK Bačka and Dinamo Pančevo before he went abroad. He joined Young Africans from Tanzania in the summer of 2008 and stayed with the club until 2011. Later he was with Azam, and after that he moved to Vietnam, in Ho Chi Minh City. Next he was with Mladost Podgorica in Montenegro for the 2012–13 season, before he moved back to Africa in 2014.
