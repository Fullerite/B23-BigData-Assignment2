Erik Oskar Siewert Öholm, (born 3 February 1980) is a Swedish politician for the Moderate Party. He is a suppleant for the National banks board, and an MP in the Riksdagen between 2006 and 2014. He is the son of television presenter and journalist Siewert Öholm. His mother Ann-Britt Öholm died of breast cancer in November 2004. == References == Category:Living people Category:1980 births
