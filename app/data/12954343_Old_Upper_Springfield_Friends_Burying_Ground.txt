Old Upper Springfield Friends Burying Ground is a cemetery located in Springfield Township and Wrightstown, in Burlington County, New Jersey. The cemetery and the accompanying meeting house were placed on both the New Jersey (state ID # 875) and the National Register of Historic Places (Reference # 79001479) in 1979.New Jersey and National Registers of Historic Places , New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection - Historic Preservation Office, updated August 6, 2007.
