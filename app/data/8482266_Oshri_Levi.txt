Oshri Levi, (, born 15 January 1974) is an Israeli footballer. As of 2013 Oshri plays football for Hapoel Karmiel in Liga Gimel and also plays basketball for Hapoel Ma'a lot in Liga Alef He played for Hapoel Kfar Saba in the Israeli Premier League, but was sacked by the club on the suspicion that he threw a match against Maccabi Haifa.
