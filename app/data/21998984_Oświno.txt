Oświno () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Chociwel, within Stargard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately north-east of Chociwel, north-east of Stargard, and east of the regional capital Szczecin. It is situated on the western shore of Woświn lake, in the historic region of Pomerania.
